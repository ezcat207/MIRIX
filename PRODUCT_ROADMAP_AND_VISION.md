# Product Roadmap & Vision: The Engine of Inevitable Success
## —— From "Recording Reality" to "Guaranteeing Outcome"

### 1. The Ultimate Goal (终局目标)
**To build an "External Prefrontal Cortex" that makes success (for Entrepreneurs & Students) inevitable.**

It is not just a "tool" (like Notion or Jira); it is a **System of Evolution**.
*   **For the Entrepreneur**: It minimizes the "Time to Product-Market Fit". It kills bad ideas fast and scales good ideas faster.
*   **For the Student**: It maximizes "Knowledge Retention per Hour". It turns passive information into active mastery.

**The End State**: A system that knows your goals better than you do, monitors your execution without bias, and intervenes *before* you fail.

---

### 2. The Four Stages of Evolution (中间阶段设计)

#### Phase 1: The Digital Twin (数字化身) —— "I know what you did."
*   **Core Function**: **Total Recall**. Passive recording of all screen activity, OCR, and interactions.
*   **User Value**: "I never lose context." Search anything, anytime.
*   **Success Metric (North Star)**: **Data Completeness & Retrieval Speed**.
    *   *Metric*: Can the user find "that thing I saw 3 weeks ago" in < 5 seconds?
    *   *Status*: **Achieved (Current MIRIX)**.

#### Phase 2: The Strategic Mirror (复盘之镜) —— "I know why you failed."
*   **Core Function**: **Reflection & Alignment**. Comparing "Actual Behavior" (Screen Data) vs. "Strategic Goals" (User Input).
*   **User Value**: "I stop lying to myself." Brutal honesty about time usage and strategic drift.
*   **Key Features**:
    *   Goal Reasoning Engine (Why are we doing this?)
    *   Automated Daily/Weekly Reviews (What went wrong?)
    *   Principle Codex (Building the rulebook).
*   **Success Metric**: **Insight Rate ("Aha!" Moments)**.
    *   *Metric*: How many times per week does the user change their behavior based on a system insight? (e.g., "I stopped doing X because MIRIX showed it was low leverage.")

#### Phase 3: The Adaptive Coach (自适应教练) —— "I know how you can win."
*   **Core Function**: **Proactive Intervention**. The system doesn't just look back (Review); it looks forward (Predict).
*   **User Value**: "I don't get stuck." The system detects confusion or procrastination and offers help *in real-time*.
*   **Key Features**:
    *   **Just-in-Time Knowledge**: "You're coding in Python? Here is the specific library documentation you need right now."
    *   **Pattern Warning**: "You are falling into the 'Perfectionism Trap' again (spending 3h on UI). Stop."
    *   **Dynamic Roadmap**: Automatically adjusting the path based on learning speed/market feedback.
*   **Success Metric**: **Goal Velocity**.
    *   *Metric*: Reduction in time required to achieve a standard milestone (e.g., "Time to MVP" or "Time to Master Concept X").

#### Phase 4: The Autonomous Partner (自主合伙人) —— "I did this for you."
*   **Core Function**: **Agentic Execution**. The system takes over the "Low Leverage" work so you can focus on "High Leverage" Strategy/Creativity.
*   **User Value**: "I clone myself."
*   **Key Features**:
    *   **Auto-Research**: "While you slept, I researched 5 competitors and summarized their pricing strategies."
    *   **Auto-Coding**: "I wrote the boilerplate code for the new feature you planned."
    *   **Auto-Testing**: "I tested your knowledge and found 3 weak points. Here is a quiz."
*   **Success Metric**: **Offload Ratio**.
    *   *Metric*: % of total work hours performed by the AI vs. the Human.
